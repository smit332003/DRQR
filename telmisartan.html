Details on Telmisartan:

Learn about Medicine

Overview

Telmisartan is an angiotensin II receptor blocker (ARB) used primarily for treating hypertension (high blood pressure) and reducing the risk of cardiovascular events.

Benefits

- Blood Pressure Control: Effectively lowers blood pressure by blocking the action of angiotensin II.

- Cardiovascular Protection: May reduce the risk of stroke, heart attack, and other cardiovascular events.

Side Effects

Common side effects include dizziness, lightheadedness, and flu-like symptoms. Serious side effects are rare but may include kidney problems.

Medication Counseling

- Take as directed by your healthcare provider.

- Regular blood pressure monitoring is important.

Contraindications

- Pregnancy: Not recommended during pregnancy, especially in the second and third trimesters.

- Severe liver disease or biliary obstruction.

- Known hypersensitivity to telmisartan or other ARBs.
