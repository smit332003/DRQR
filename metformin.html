Details on Metformin:

Learn about Medicine

Overview

Metformin is a first-line medication for managing type 2 diabetes. It belongs to the biguanide class and works by improving the body's response to insulin, reducing the amount of glucose produced by the liver, and increasing the uptake of glucose by cells.

Benefits

Blood Sugar Control: Metformin helps lower blood sugar levels by improving insulin sensitivity.

Weight Management: It may contribute to weight loss, which is beneficial for those with type 2 diabetes.

Side Effects

Gastrointestinal issues (nausea, diarrhea, abdominal discomfort). Serious side effects are rare but may include lactic acidosis, a potentially life-threatening condition.

Medication Counseling

Take with meals to minimize gastrointestinal side effects.

Regular blood glucose monitoring is important.

Contraindications

Severe kidney disease.

History of lactic acidosis.
