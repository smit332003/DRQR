Details on Chloroquin Phosphate:

Learn about Medicine

Overview

Chloroquine phosphate is an antimalarial medication that has been used for decades. It works by interfering with the growth of parasites in red blood cells.

Benefits

- Malaria Treatment and Prevention: Effectively treats and prevents malaria caused by certain parasites.

- Investigated Uses: In some cases, it has been investigated for potential use in autoimmune diseases due to its anti-inflammatory properties.

Side Effects

Common side effects include nausea, headache, and stomach cramps. Serious side effects are rare but may include vision changes and

Medication Counseling

- Take as prescribed by your healthcare provider.

- Inform your doctor of any pre-existing health conditions.

- Regular eye exams may be recommended.

Contraindications

- History of certain eye problems.

- Known hypersensitivity to chloroquine or related drugs.

- History of heart disease.
