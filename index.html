Intentions of Dr QR:

Scan for Knowledge:

After purchasing medicine, simply scan the QR code located next to the medication strip.

This initial step ensures a seamless connection between your medication and our information platform.

Access Information Anytime, Anywhere:

Visit our website to access detailed information about your medication in multiple languages.

Our user-friendly platform provides comprehensive details, including dosage, side effects, and usage instructions.

Empower Your Health Journey:

Step into a world of empowerment where information is key.

By scanning and accessing our website, you take control of your health journey with knowledge, ensuring informed decisions about your well-being.





Medications

Metformin

Purpose: Metformin helps control blood sugar levels. It's commonly prescribed for type 2 diabetes patients, particularly when diet and exercise alone have not been sufficient to control their blood glucose levels.

Telmisartan

Purpose: Telmisartan is used to treat high blood pressure by relaxing blood vessels. It falls under the category of angiotensin II receptor blockers (ARBs) and may also be prescribed for managing cardiovascular conditions.

Chloroquin Phosphate

Purpose: Chloroquine phosphate is mainly used to prevent and treat malaria by inhibiting the growth of the malaria parasite. It has also been considered for treating autoimmune diseases like rheumatoid arthritis and lupus due to its anti-inflammatory properties.
